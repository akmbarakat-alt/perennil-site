OUR STORY

6,000 years
of Egyptian hands.

Egypt was among the first civilizations on earth to shape clay into daily life. From the banks of the Nile, generations of artisans carried the same material through Pharaonic, Nubian, Greek, Roman, Islamic and Ottoman eras.

PERENNIL continues this legacy through contemporary handmade pottery crafted in Egypt. Every piece reflects the memory of the river, the warmth of the kiln and the continuity of human hands shaping earth into culture.
